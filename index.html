About Me

I am a Ph.D. student at the School of Cybersecurity, Korea University, specializing in post-quantum cryptography(PQC). My research focuses on designing secure and efficient PQC schemes and studying how they can be effectively integrated into real-world systems, such as communication protocols. I am particularly interested in the intersection of cryptographic theory and its practical deployment, including applications in secure communications and post-quantum network architectures. I aim to contribute to building cryptographic systems that remain secure in the quantum era, with both strong theoretical foundations and practical applicability.
